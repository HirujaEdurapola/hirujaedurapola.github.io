About Me

Code by day.
Stars by night.

I'm Hiruja Edurapola — known as SJ. A 15-year-old builder from Sri Lanka who spends weekdays writing code, weekends stargazing, and every spare moment pushing pixels until something looks interesting.

I study at Sri Sumangala College, Panadura, and I founded Hiruja Studios and SJ Research Labs as my personal space to experiment, break things, and figure out how they work. I believe technology should be beautiful, fast, and built with curiosity.
