SJ Trainer

SJ Trainer is a free, ad-free chess training tool that offers users positions from real games for practice. Unlike traditional puzzles, it provides unbiased challenges with no preset advantages or disadvantages. Simply visit the website, analyze the position displayed, calculate your move, and enter it.
